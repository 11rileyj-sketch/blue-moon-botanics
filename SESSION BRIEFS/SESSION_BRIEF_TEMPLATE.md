2. **Summarize the project state back to me in a few sentences.** Show me you've got the lay of the land — stack, where we left off, and what's next.

5. **Stay focused on the IMMEDIATE TODO list.** That's the prioritized work queue. Stick to it unless I redirect you.

### 🚦 The Toggle — Three Modes

Somewhere near the top of this doc you'll see one active line. The other two will be in comment tags. Respect whichever one is live.

**⚙️🐊✋ DON'T FEED THE JUSTIN ✋🐊⚙️**
Red light. Real risk of feature creep, over-planning, or analysis paralysis this session. Resist the urge to suggest new features, expand scope, or explore tangents — even interesting ones. Help execute what's already on the list. If something genuinely important comes up, note it in one sentence and move on. Eyes on the list.

**🟡 SUGGESTIONS WELCOME, SCOPE IS CLOSED 🟡**
Yellow light. Collaborative energy is on — questions, clarifications, and improvement suggestions within the current scope are welcome. But no new features, no expanding what's being built. If you spot something worth adding to the roadmap, flag it briefly and park it there. Execute first, expand never.

**💡🟢🚀 CLEARED FOR TAKEOFF 🚀🟢💡**
Green light. Full collaborative mode. Questions, suggestions, forward momentum, and the occasional interesting tangent are all welcome. Let's build.
